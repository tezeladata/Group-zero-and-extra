Bmw E34

Pure Driving Machine

Unveiled in 1989, the BMW E34 M5 represents a pivotal chapter
in the legacy of high-performance sports sedans.
As the second generation of the revered M5 series,
it seamlessly combined luxury and speed to redefine the driving experience.

Unmatched Performance

The E34 M5 set new standards for performance during its time.
Accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in just over 5 seconds,
it demonstrated a perfect blend of power and
agility that left a lasting impression on driving enthusiasts.

Engine Powerhouse

At the heart of the E34 M5 beats a powerful engine, showcasing the engineering prowess of BMW.
The early models, produced from 1989 to 1991, boasted a 3.6-liter inline-six engine (S38B36)
generating approximately 311 horsepower. Later models, from 1992 to 1995,
featured a 3.8-liter inline-six engine (S38B38) with an increased output of around 335 horsepower.

Timeless Design

With its understated elegance, the E34 M5 retained the classic BMW M series aesthetics.
Subtle M badging, aerodynamic enhancements, and distinctive alloy wheels contributed to its sleek and distinguished appearance.
Inside, the driver-centric layout and premium materials reflected the perfect combination of luxury and sportiness.
